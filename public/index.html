Welcome to UltraCoach AI Bot

About This Bot

This is a proof-of-concept AI bot built on the DigitalOcean GenAI stack. It uses hundreds of ultrarunning podcast transcripts as its knowledge base, powering a sophisticated DO GenAI RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) solution.

What Can It Do?

The UltraCoach AI Bot can answer questions, provide insights, and discuss topics related to ultrarunning, endurance coaching, and sports science. It's like having a virtual ultrarunning coach at your fingertips!

How to Use

Look for the chat icon in the lower right corner of your screen. Click on it to open the chat interface and start interacting with the UltraCoach AI Bot. Feel free to ask questions about training, racing strategies, or any topic related to ultrarunning and endurance sports.
